Meet us...

Hello, we are Ūla and Viltė, the creators of maziau.lt. We are from Vilnius, Lithuania. We came up with beeswax wrap idea during the world pandemic in 2020 and our mission is to reduce single-use waste in the household by replacing it with more sustainable products. A word "mažiau" in Lithuanian means less. And we think that less is more. In the world that is so worn out by our impact on the planet we aim to come up with solutions to reduce our footprint and minimise the use of single-use plastic. We aim to teach people and offer them a better solution for everyday food storage used in their home such as handmade reusable beeswax wraps. We want the world community to find healthier and more sustainable ways to store and use their food, as well as reduce global warming and make a positive impact by minimising ocean pollution across the globe. Our beeswax wraps are hand made by us and are made of cotton, natural beeswax, resin and jojoba oil which is known for its anti-bacterial properties, therefore, it is safe to use with food products.
